Specializations

At levels 1, 7, and 18, trainers may choose a specialization from the following list, granting them bonuses depending on the type of Pokemon they train. Specializations can be stacked for an additional +1 bonus to skill checks each time it is chosen.

Options

Poké Fan

normal

Increase your CHA by 1, to a maximum of 20. Add a +1 bonus to all skill checks made by any of your Normal-type Pokémon.
