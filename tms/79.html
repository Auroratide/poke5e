79 - Frost Breath

ice
Move Power
con, dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
5
Duration
instantaneous
Range
15ft

You blow a gust of freezing cold air at a creature in range. Make a ranged attack roll, dealing 1d12 + MOVE ice damage on a hit. This move scores a critical hit on natural attack rolls of 16 and higher.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d8 at level 5, 2d12 at level 10, and 6d6 at level 17.
