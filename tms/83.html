83 - Infestation

bug
Move Power
dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
5
Duration
3 rounds, concentration
Range
40ft

You summon an infestation of tiny insects to attack a creature for multiple turns. Make a ranged attack on a target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 + MOVE bug damage and becomes infested with swarming bugs. As long as you maintain concentration, the target takes an additional 1d8 bug damage at the end of each of its next three turns.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice rolls for this move changes to 2d6 at level 5, 4d4 at level 10, and 3d10 at level 17.
