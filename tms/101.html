101 - Pounce

bug
Move Power
dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
10
Duration
instantaneous
Range
melee

You pounce at the target and leap away. Make a melee attack roll against a target, doing 1d8 + MOVE bug damage on hit. If you hit, you may move an additional 10ft without invoking an attack of opportunity.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d6 at level 5, 4d4 at level 10, and 3d10 at level 17.
