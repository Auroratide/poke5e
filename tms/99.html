99 - Dazzling Gleam

fairy
Move Power
dex, cha
Move Time
1 action
PP
5
Duration
instantaneous
Range
self (20ft radius)

You emit a powerful flash that damages all creatures in range. Creatures within 20 feet of you must make a CON save against your Move DC, taking 2d6 + MOVE fairy damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d8 at level 5, 4d6 at level 10, and 6d6 at level 17.
