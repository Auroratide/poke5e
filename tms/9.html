9 - Venoshock

poison
Move Power
dex, con
Move Time
1 action
PP
5
Duration
instantaneous
Range
15ft

You attempt to drench a creature in poisonous liquid. Make a ranged attack on a creature, dealing 1d10 + MOVE poison damage on a hit. If the target is already poisoned, double the dice roll for the damage

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d6 at level 5, 2d12 at level 10, and 3d10 at level 17.
