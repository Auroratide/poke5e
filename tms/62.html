62 - Acrobatics

flying
Move Power
dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
5
Duration
instantaneous
Range
melee

You nimbly attack a target with acrobatic prowess. Make a melee attack, dealing 3d6 + MOVE flying damage on a hit. If you are holding an item when you activate this move, the damage is halved.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 3d8 at level 5, 6d6 at level 10, and 7d8 at level 17.
