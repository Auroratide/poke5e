84 - Poison Jab

poison
Move Power
str, dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
10
Duration
instantaneous
Range
melee

You stab a creature with an appendage steeped in poison. Make a melee attack on a creature, dealing 2d6 + MOVE poison damage on a hit. On a natural attack roll of 16 or higher, the target is poisoned.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d8 at level 5, 4d6 at level 10, and 6d6 at level 17.
