8 - Bulk Up

fighting
Move Power
none
Move Time
1 action
PP
10
Duration
1 minute, concentration
Range
self

You pump yourself up, increasing your adrenaline. For the next minute, choose to add +1 to your attack rolls OR +1 to AC. This move can be stacked to a maximum of +5 for either choice.
