Shadow Claw

ghost

Info

Move Power
str, dex
Move Time
1 action
PP
10
Duration
instantaneous
Range
20ft

You strike with a sharp claw made from shadows in range. Make a ranged attack, dealing 1d12 + MOVE ghost damage on a hit. This moves scores a critical hit on natural attack rolls of 19 and 20.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 2d8 at level 5, 2d12 at level 10, and 6d6 at level 17.
