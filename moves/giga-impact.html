normal

Info

str
Move Time
1 action, recharge
PP
3
Duration
instantaneous
Range
melee

You charge at a creature with every ounce of your power. Make a melee attack, dealing 3d8 + MOVE normal damage on a hit. This move cannot be used on consecutive turns by the same creature.

At Higher Levels: The damage dice roll for this move changes to 5d6 at level 5, 4d12 at level 10, and 8d8 at level 17.
