The Jolly Pokémon. Lombre is nocturnal-it will get active after dusk. It is also a mischief maker. When this Pokémon spots anglers, it tugs on their fishing lines from beneath the surface and enjoys their consternation.

Swift Swim: This Pokémon's speed is doubled in rainy conditions.

Rain Dish: In rainy conditions, this Pokémon heals for an amount of HP equal to its proficiency bonus at the end of each of its turns.

Own Tempo: This Pokémon is immune to becoming confused.

Evolution: 2 / 3

Lombre can evolve into Ludicolo at level 10 and above with the help of a Water Stone. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 7 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
