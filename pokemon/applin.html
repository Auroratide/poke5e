Ripen: Doubles the effects of berries consumed.

Gluttony: This Pokémon must eat its held berry when it falls below ½ of its maximum HP.

Bulletproof: This Pokémon is immune to damage from moves with Bullet, Ball, or Bomb in their name.

Evolution: 1 / 2

Applin can evolve at level 5 and above. It evolves into a Flapple if inhabiting a green or gold apple, and into an Appletun if inhabiting a red apple. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 14 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
