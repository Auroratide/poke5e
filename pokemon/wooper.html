Damp: This Pokémon is unaffected by Self Destruct and Explosion moves.

Water Absorb: This Pokémon takes no damage from water or water-type attacks. Instead, half of any water damage done is absorbed, restoring the Pokémon's HP.

Unaware: When this Pokémon attacks an opponent, it ignores any stat changes the opponent has been affected with after the start of battle.

Evolution: 1 / 2

Wooper can evolve into Quagsire at level 6 and above. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 9 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
