Hustle: When you score a critical hit, you may immediately gain an additional action on your turn. If this action is used to make an attack, you have disadvantage on the roll. You may only ever have one additional action per round.

Serene Grace: The Move DC to pass a saving throw against one of this Pokémon's moves is increased by 1.

Super Luck: This Pokémon increases its critical hit range by 1 (20 to 19+, etc.)

Evolution: 2 / 3

Togetic can evolve into Togekiss at level 15 and above with the help of a Shiny Stone. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 15 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
