Torrent: This Pokémon doubles its STAB bonus when it has 25% or less of its maximum health.

Sheer Force: When activating a move that has a chance to impose a stat change or inflict a status, this creature instead doubles its move modifier for the damage with no chance of the additional effect.

Evolution: 2 / 3

Croconaw can evolve into Feraligatr at level 14 and above. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 10 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
