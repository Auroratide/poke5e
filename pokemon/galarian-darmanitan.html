Gorilla Tactics: When this Pokémon enters the battlefield, choose one of its moves. Double the MOVE damage modifier for the selected move; the MOVE damage modifier for this Pokémon's other moves becomes 0. The effect resets when this Pokémon leaves the battlefield.
