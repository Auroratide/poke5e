Rattled: When this Pokémon is hit by a damaging Dark, Bug, or Ghost move, it makes its next attack at advantage.

Static: When this Pokémon is hit by a melee attack, roll a d4. On a result of 4, the attacker takes an amount of electric damage equal to this Pokemon's proficiency bonus.

Klutz: This Pokémon cannot hold an item.

Evolution: 1 / 2

Toxel can evolve into Toxtricity at level 8 and above. It takes on the Amped Form if its nature is Arrogant, Brave, Energetic, Grumpy, Hardy, Hasty, Naughty, Rash, Reckless, or Sassy. Otherwise, it takes on the Low Key form. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 11 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
