The Fly Scorpion Pokémon. Gligar glides through the air without a sound as if it were sliding. This Pokémon hangs on to the face of its foe using its clawed hind legs and the large pincers on its forelegs, then injects the prey with its poison barb.

Gligar can evolve into Gliscor at level 10 and above while holding a Razor Fang at night. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 9 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
