Cute Charm: Once per short rest, you can impose disadvantage on an enemy attack roll of your choice.

Magic Guard: If this Pokémon is subjected to a move that forces it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage on a success.

Friend Guard: Once per long rest, when an ally within 15 feet of this Pokémon is hit by an attack, it may choose to halve the damage dealt.

Evolution: 2 / 3

Clefairy can evolve into Clefable at level 8 and above with the help of a Moon Stone. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 11 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
