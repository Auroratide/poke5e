Cheek Pouch: When this Pokémon eats any type of berry, is heals for an additional 10% of its maximum HP (rounded up to the nearest whole number).

Gluttony: This Pokémon must eat its held berry when it falls below ½ of its maximum HP.

Evolution: 1 / 2

Skwovet can evolve into Greedent at level 6 and above. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 10 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
