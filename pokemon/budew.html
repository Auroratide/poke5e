Natural Cure: This Pokémon is cured of negative status ailments upon returning to its Pokeball.

Poison Point: When this Pokémon is hit with a melee attack, roll a 1d4. On a result of 4, the attacker takes an amount of poison damage equal to this Pokémon’s proficiency modifier.

Leaf Guard: This Pokémon does not suffer from any negative status ailments in harsh sunlight.

Evolution: 1 / 3

Budew can evolve into Roselia at level 6 and above if its Loyalty level is +2 or higher. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 10 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
