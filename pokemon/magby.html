The Live Coal Pokémon. Magby's state of health is determined by observing the fire it breathes. If the Pokémon is spouting yellow flames from its mouth, it is in good health. When it is fatigued, black smoke will be mixed in with the flames.

Flame Body: The flames from this Pokémon's body shine dim light in a 15 ft radius. In addition, when hit by a melee attack, roll a d10. On a 10, the attacker is burned.

Vital Spirit: This Pokémon cannot be put to sleep.

Evolution: 1 / 3

Magby can evolve into Magmar at level 8 and above if its Loyalty level is +2 or higher. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 8 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
