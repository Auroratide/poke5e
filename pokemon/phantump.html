Natural Cure: This Pokémon is cured of negative status ailments upon returning to its Pokeball.

Frisk: Upon entering a battle, a single opponent’s held item is revealed, if it has one.

Harvest: At the end of this Pokémon's turn, if it used a berry, roll 1d4. On a result of 3 or 4, it regains that berry as a held item.

Evolution: 1 / 2

Phantump can evolve into Trevenant at level 10 and above. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 11 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
