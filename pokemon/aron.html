Sturdy: When taking damage equal to half or more of your current HP, roll a d4. On a result of 3 of 4, halve the damage dealt.

Rock Head: This Pokémon takes no recoil damage.

Heavy Metal: This Pokémon is especially heavy. Once per long rest, after being targeted by an attack, it may increase its AC by 2 if it would cause an attack to miss.

Evolution: 1 / 3

Aron can evolve into Lairon at level 8 and above. When it evolves, its health increases by double its level, and it gains 11 points to add to its ability scores (max 20).
