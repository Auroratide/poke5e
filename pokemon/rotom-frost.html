Levitate: This Pokémon is immune to ground moves.

Blizzard: This Pokémon knows Blizzard as a fifth move. The move is forgotten if it takes any other form.

Form Change (Rotom): Rotom has 5 alternate forms that it can take when it is near a household appliance. As a bonus action, Rotom can take the Heat Form, Wash Form, Frost Form, Fan Form, or Mow Form, changing its typing and stats. The form is dependent on the type of appliance it possesses (DM Discretion). separate stat blocks are provided for each form.
